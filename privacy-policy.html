Controller

2. Interactions with our AI Assistant (Reception 24/7)

To provide you with fast and efficient service, incoming calls and web-based voice interactions enquiring about our services are handled by Reception 24/7 by Elevated Business, an AI-powered automated voice technology.

When you interact with our AI assistant, please be aware of the following:

  • Automated Interaction: You are speaking with an automated AI assistant, not a human operator.
  • Transient Processing: Information you provide during the call is processed on a transient basis to facilitate your enquiry, answer your questions about our services, or take a message.
  • Call Summaries: Text summaries and transcripts of your conversation are generated from the interaction to assist our human team with administrative record-keeping and to follow up on your enquiry.
  • No Sensitive Data: We ask that you do not provide sensitive personal data (such as health information or payment card details) to the AI assistant unless explicitly requested for a specific, secure transaction.

3. The Data We Collect About You

We may collect, use, store, and transfer different kinds of personal data about you, including:

  • Identity Data: First name, last name, or business name.
  • Contact Data: Telephone number, email address, and billing/delivery addresses.
  • Communication Data: Voice audio (processed transiently), text transcripts, and Call Summaries generated by the AI assistant.
  • Technical Data: Caller ID (CLI), IP address, browser type, and operating system when you use our website.

4. How We Use Your Personal Data

We will only use your personal data when the law allows us to. Most commonly, we will use your personal data in the following circumstances:

  • Performance of a Contract: To take steps at your request before entering into a contract with you (e.g., providing quotes or information about our voice AI services).
  • Legitimate Interests: For our legitimate business interests (e.g., keeping records of enquiries, improving our AI assistant's responses, and managing our business administration).
  • Legal Obligation: Where we need to comply with a legal or regulatory obligation.

5. Disclosures of Your Personal Data

We do not sell your personal data. However, to provide our AI reception service, your data may be securely routed to and processed by carefully selected third parties:

  • AI and Infrastructure Providers: Third-party technology providers engaged to support the underlying delivery and processing of the Reception 24/7 service.
  • Business Integrations: External business tools (such as scheduling platforms like Calendly, or CRM systems) that we use to book appointments or manage customer relationships.

All third-party service providers are required to respect the security of your personal data and treat it in accordance with the law and specific Data Processing Agreements (DPAs).

6. International Transfers

Some of our external third parties (such as our AI infrastructure providers) may be based outside the UK. Whenever we transfer your personal data out of the UK, we ensure a similar degree of protection is afforded to it by using specific contracts approved for use in the UK (such as Standard Contractual Clauses or the UK Addendum).

7. Data Retention

We will only retain your personal data for as long as reasonably necessary to fulfil the purposes we collected it for, including for the purposes of satisfying any legal, regulatory, tax, accounting, or reporting requirements.

  • Call Summaries and Transcripts: Retained for 6 months following your enquiry.
  • Customer Records: Retained for the duration of your contract with us and for 5 years thereafter for legal and tax purposes.

8. Your Legal Rights

Under UK data protection laws, you have rights in relation to your personal data, including the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data.
  • Request correction of the personal data that we hold about you.
  • Request erasure of your personal data.
  • Object to processing of your personal data.
  • Request restriction of processing of your personal data.
  • Request the transfer of your personal data to you or to a third party.

If you wish to exercise any of the rights set out above, please contact us.

You also have the right to make a complaint at any time to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO), the UK supervisory authority for data protection (www.ico.org.uk). We would, however, appreciate the chance to deal with your concerns before you approach the ICO.
